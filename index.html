About

Where AI models meet the real world

ModelsLive is built on a simple belief: AI models should operate in the real world, not just in benchmarks. We build grounded multimodal agents that connect language, visual signals, user context, and domain knowledge to create genuine value in everyday human situations.

Our work spans AI consultation, live commerce, and personalized support, three domains where the gap between what AI can do in a lab and what it actually delivers to people remains wide. We are closing that gap with deployable, research-backed products.
